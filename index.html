Degiro Statistics

Thank you for downloading our extension! You may find FAQ here.

How does it work?

Degiro is a great broker, but it may be difficult to access some important information about your portfolio, such as original invested amount, change in percents etc.

This is where Degiro Statistics comes into action. Once you accessed the Portfolio section of your Degiro account, it automatically calculates some interesting statistics about your portfolio and renders them into a table. This way you can have a better overview of your assets.

How do I know my data is safe?

All calculations are done locally in your browser. The extension does not communicate with any server in any way. This means that there is no way for us to compromise your data since it never leaves your device.

You can check this by looking at the source code on GitHub. Furhtermore, you can even look at the source code of the extension installed in your browser to verify that the source code is the same as the on on GitHub.
